Who We Are ?

The E-Commerce Association, the first of its kind in the Kingdom, combines innovation, training, and support to achieve a significant leap in Saudi Arabia's digital market. Saudi Arabia Vision 2030It focuses on solidifying values, enhancing knowledge, and improving equal access to education. The aim is to provide a space where newcomers and experienced peers can interact, inspire each other, and promote open scientific practices and values in their workflow. It also seeks to enhance policies that support innovation and sustainability in the Kingdom's e-commerce market.

About the Association

The E-Commerce Association is a professional civil society organization established under Ministerial Decision No. 10146. We aim to support innovation, enhance digital capabilities, and connect entrepreneurs with opportunities in the Saudi digital market.

Vision

Leadership and innovation in the e-commerce market, with a focus on supporting individuals, institutions, and the community.
Our members are in constant communication and collaboration with communities in Europe and the Arab world.
Working with a team spirit.
Continuous development.

Mission

To be the driving force in enhancing e-commerce by providing awareness, training, and local and international partnerships.

The Impact of the Association

Creating new digital jobs.
Improving the quality of life.
Supporting youth and women in the labor market.

Sponsors

Your support as a sponsor of the association will have a significant impact in realizing your social responsibility, supporting purposeful volunteer work, and contributing actively to the nonprofit sector in the Kingdom. It will also help create numerous job opportunities in the labor market for citizens, support entrepreneurs and talented individuals, and aid in fulfilling their aspirations, achieving their goals and dreams, nurturing their innovations, and advancing them. This will positively contribute to strengthening the e-commerce ecosystem in the Kingdom and supporting the national economy. The association's vision intersects with Saudi Vision 2030 in over 47% of its objectives. Sponsoring the association will support these goals, which cover various social, economic, and technical areas. It will also specifically contribute to enhancing the identity of the Saudi people by launching numerous innovative national projects in the e-commerce field. This emerging sector has had a significant impact on consumer behavior globally, with the COVID-19 crisis serving as a major testament to the strength of this market and its ability to withstand various risks. It has also redirected the focus of traditional trade toward it and represented inspiring success stories.

Why the Association?

Excellence: The association enjoys unique privileges, including government support and tax exemptions. Local and International Recognition: Through its legal status, the association has gained official recognition both within the Kingdom and abroad. Supportive Scientific and Practical Environment: The association fosters, mentors, educates, and trains entrepreneurs and technologists. Research Keeping Up with Development: It collects, provides, updates, and tracks all information related to e-commerce. Community Service: By developing innovative ideas and supporting volunteer activities.

Official sponsor page

Partner Sponsor Page

Privileges: The sponsorship benefits are designed to serve the sponsor's objectives and include the following aspects: The association's website and its affiliated platforms. Host Partner

Host Partner It is a temporary partnership associated with a specific event with legal entities that provide services directly linked to the event or contribute effectively to its success in collaboration with the association. This partnership lasts throughout the duration of the event unless both parties agree otherwise. Privileges: The sponsorship benefits are tailored to serve the sponsor's objectives and are focused on aspects related to the event, including: The association's electronic platform and its branches. The association's social media accounts. Marketing and awareness media materials.
