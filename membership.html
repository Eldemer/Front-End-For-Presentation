Memberships

E-Commerce Association | A Community for Business Leaders – Regular, Honorary, and Distinguished Memberships to Foster Growth and Innovation

Honorary Membership It is the highest level of membership offered by the association to prominent and influential figures in the Kingdom with legal status, given the significant moral value their membership brings. It is granted by the Chairman of the Board of Directors following a decision by the Board. Benefits of Honorary Membership: Attendance at the association's general meetings. Access to quarterly results. Access to periodic reports. A dedicated page in the "Honorary Members" section, including a biography. Conditions for Obtaining Honorary Membership: It is granted by the Chairman of the Board of Directors following a decision by the Board.
Honorary Membership It is a membership offered by the association to individuals with legal or natural status, given the advantages and benefits—whether moral, material, or honorary—that their membership brings. It is granted by a decision of the Board of Directors. Benefits of Honorary Membership: Attendance at the association's general meetings. Access to quarterly results. Access to periodic reports. A dedicated page in the "Honorary Members" section, including a biography. Conditions for Obtaining Honorary Membership: Financial support of no less than 15,000 Saudi Riyals annually or its equivalent in-kind support. Approval from the Board of Directors.
Regular Membership Regular Membership It is a membership for joining the E-Commerce Association, granted to individuals with natural status who are practitioners of e-commerce or one of its branches, or those interested in its various fields. Benefits of Regular Membership: Discounts on all association products. Access to exclusive member reports. Discounts on tickets for association events (courses, exhibitions, conferences). Discounts on rental spaces at the association's annual exhibition. Conditions for Obtaining Regular Membership: Filling out the application form. Payment of an annual fee of 300 Saudi Riyals.
Information that may interest you: Every riyal spent on the association returns 3 riyals to the Kingdom’s economy.
